About me

Hi :), I am Yuanxin Guo, a 3rd year undergrad from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). I am a math major student and my main research interest lies in information theory and coding theory.

I am planning to join University of Toronto this summer as a research intern. I will be supervised by Prof. Stark Draper.

I am currently doing an internship at the Wireless Technology Laboratory, Huawei Technologies since January 2021. Meanwhile I am affiliated with the Network Coding Lab at CUHK-Shenzhen and the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data as research assistants.

I have gained some teaching experience as an undergraduate student teaching fellow. The courses I have taught include Calculus I, Linear Algebra, and Complex Variables.

I am seeking for a PhD position in information theory/statistics/operations research starting in the 2022 fall. A brief CV is available here.
